Welcome to Sahid Montana Hotel

Dear Valued Customers,

“Warmest Greetings from Hotel Sahid Montana Malang & Hotel Sahid Montana Dua Malang”

Thank you for visiting our website. Allow me to briefly introduce Hotel Sahid Montana and Hotel Sahid Montana Dua Malang.

Hotel Sahid Montana Malang is a 3 star hotel completed with 70- well appointed rooms and suites, and 4 meeting rooms with various capacity. Located right in the heart of the city, it is surrounded by government offices and business area. While Hotel Sahid Montana Dua Malang which located in the sub-urban area of Malang, is well designed with 70 rooms and suite, and various capacity meeting rooms in a spacious landscape, complemented with beautiful garden and swimming pool.

Both Hotel Sahid Montana and Sahid Montana Dua Malang are well suited for leisure, meeting, gathering, exhibition, and wedding party. “Cinnamon Snack & Coffee” at Hotel Sahid Montana and “Alang – Alang Snack & Coffee “ at Hotel Sahid Montana Dua Malang are in addition to our leisure facility.

Check out our feature and other promotions in this website. We are looking forward to host you at Hotel Sahid Montana and Hotel Sahid Montana Dua Malang. Come and experience memorable stay with us.

With Hospitality Regards,

Slamet Sudiharto General Manager
